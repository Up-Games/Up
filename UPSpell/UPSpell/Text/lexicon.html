The lexicon is the game’s word list. It’s built for fun. It contains everyday words, proper names, acronyms, tech terms, texting slang, the names of Santa’s reindeer, and much more. Try stuff.
Some derisive terms are not in the lexicon. Many impolite words are. Use them or not.
