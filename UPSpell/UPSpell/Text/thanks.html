I made Up Spell by building on the work of many other people. Thanks to all.

— Ken Kocienda

Some essentials:

  • Adobe software to make art and sound.
  • PaintCode to generate code from art.
  • Ableton Live and Avid Sibelius to make tunes.
  • J.S. Bach and the people who created the old-time square dance musical tradition.
  • The people who studied the English language and built lexicons of their own. Listed below.
  • Mallory by Frere-Jones Type. Beautiful typeface.
  • xScope to line up stuff just so.
  • BBEdit to hack text. It still doesn’t suck.
  • Apple software, hardware, and developer resources.
  • The patience of my wife, Jean Gordon Kocienda. ❤️
